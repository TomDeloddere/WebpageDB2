Competency Framework for Database Administrator Level 1

This website supports the bachelor thesis on defining and developing a competency framework for the entry-level position of Database Administrator Level 1 within the z/OS mainframe industry.

Here you will find the structure of the bachelor thesis, including relevant documentation, literature studies, and a proof-of-concept web application. The focus is on developing a structured approach for educators to better prepare students for a career in the mainframe sector.

Practical, task-oriented exercises and use cases play a crucial role. These include hands-on experience with fundamental z/OS mainframe concepts such as JCL scripting, DB2 database management, and RUNSTATS optimization.

During my internship at Belfius, I collaborate with experienced mainframe professionals to refine the relevance and applicability of the framework. All materials and solutions are made available here.
